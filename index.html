A bit about me:

My name is Sara. I am Italian, from a small town close to Milan, but I am a bit Australian too. I moved to Sydney to complete my studies and lived there for almost eight years. Work then took me back to Italy (Rome), then UK, Switzerland and then back here in London. I love travelling, marine turtles and interior design. I have mainly worked for international not-for-profit organisations and my professional background is in Human Resources, more specifically HR policies.

Why coding?

I believe this is a discipline that requires a methodical, analytical way of thinking - and patience too - and that can be very rewarding when you are able to create something from start to finish and take full ownership of projects. It has been a great learning experience so far - I look forward to learning more and getting started on projects!
